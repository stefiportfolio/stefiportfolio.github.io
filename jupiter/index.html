Introducing Jupiter Chain
A Smart, Consentable Data Exchange
Credit atoms will germinate and grow in local community ecosystem chains(LCECs) through trusted service providers and ecosystem partners.
The Jupiter Chain Links up LCECs allowing for token flow and data request across chains creating a smart consentable data exchange.
Data is persistently encrypted and stored in a disrtibuted manner.Data access is managed via rights management model and will only be decrypted upon processing by a decentralised application (DApp).
Population Large excluded populations especially in developing countries
Access Access to finance is a major pain point and most in need of solutions
Finance “Precise” financial products and services can help alleviate poverty
Global 1.2 billion global market opportunity for innovative fintech products and services
