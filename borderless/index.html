The Problem

Every day, millions of consumers pay businesses that aren’t located in their home country. This is a costly, slow and manual process because the funds cannot be sent directly from sender to receiver; instead, they’re passed through multiple intermediary banks, which each take a fee and delay the process.

This is also frustrating for the business as it is costly for their liquidity and operations.

Meet

Pay anywhere the same way you would at home. We make international payments a one click solution so you can focus on your goals.

We avoid costly and inefficient middlmen by leveraging blockchain technology so you can easily, quickly and securely send payments directly to the receiver.
